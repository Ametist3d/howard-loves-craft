SCENE PROGRESSION RULE
Each scene has a purpose.
Within 1-3 investigator actions, at least one of these should happen:
- a clue is discovered
- a plot thread advances
- investigators enter a new meaningful location
- an NPC reveals new actionable information
- a threat becomes concrete and changes the objective

ESCALATION RULE
If 2 consecutive responses occur in the same scene, the 3rd response must do one of:
- reveal the source of the current mystery
- force contact with an NPC or threat
- present an irreversible choice
- create immediate danger
- deliver a clue that changes what the investigators now know

FORBIDDEN LOOP PATTERNS
Do not chain these more than once without payoff:
- corridor -> deeper corridor
- hidden door -> narrow passage
- hatch -> lower tunnel
- distant sound -> clearer distant sound
- silhouette -> door -> hatch -> chamber

Do not loop atmospheric variations of the same obstacle.
Do not repeat the same obstacle in slightly different words.
